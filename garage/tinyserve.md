The problem: you vibe coded a service that only your family and friends need. What if you have 10 of such projects? Or 50?

Host it from home. Accept the trade-offs.

Even 16GB of RAM of Mac Mini is enough. Nowadays it's easier than ever to write a program with very low overhead and memory footprint, because it's tailored to you: less features and less dependencies. And in languages like Go or Rust, with the single contained binary.
